THUG OF WORD ' S WAR

DEBATE!

1st round

~The registered participants will be paired with another participant randomly.

~A single topic will be given to each pair and one will be asked to speak the positive(pros) and the other negative(cons) side of the given topic.

2nd round

~12 participants will be shortlisted from the first round.

~The shortlisted participants will be formed into 4 groups(3 members for each group) and different topics will be given to the groups.

~The 3 members from each group have to speak on the pros, cons and the conclusion of the given topic respectively.

Final Round

~The top 2 groups will be selected.

~ Head to Head competition will be conducted between the two groups and the top 3 participants will be awarded the final 3 prizes.
